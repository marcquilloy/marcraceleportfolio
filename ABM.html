Accountancy, Business, and Management (ABM)

The Accountancy, Business, and Management (ABM) Strand prepares students for entrepreneurship, finance, marketing, and corporate management careers. It equips learners with essential business planning, financial analysis, and leadership skills while fostering innovation and strategic thinking. ABM is ideal for aspiring business leaders and professionals who aim to excel in the corporate and entrepreneurial sectors.
