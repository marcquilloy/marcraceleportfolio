Humanities and Social Sciences (HUMSS)

The Humanities and Social Sciences (HUMSS) Strand focuses on developing critical thinking, communication, and analytical skills. It prepares students for careers in education, journalism, psychology, politics, and social work. With an emphasis on human behavior, culture, and society, HUMSS equips learners to become future leaders, advocates, and professionals in social and community development.
